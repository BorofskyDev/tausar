FAQ
Have any questions?

What do you do?

We specialize in Search and Rescue, Evacuations, and Humanitarian Aid delivery with 24-hour logistical support for our Driver+Copilot Teams.

How do you know where to go and when?

We source knowledge of pickup points from our broad contacts, as well as from local level governments in each Oblast. We keep our vehicles supplied by the TAU supply team and we have built up a fuel depot with canisters at our rear operations base. We analyze the news and work with partners for updates on dangers on the ground.

According to the UN, only 5,000 civilians have died in Ukraine. Is it honestly that bad over there?

The United Nations is not presenting the numbers accurately or even in good faith. Over 50,000 Ukrainian civilians have died in this war and over 1.4 million have been deported from occupied Ukraine into Russia, including 320,000 children. You can help stop this murder and kidnapping.

Does my money really make a difference?

Dollar for dollar we are about as efficient as it gets. We evacuate at the same rate as several other organizations with ten times the vehicle fleet. We have not lost a single vehicle and have repaired several. We have not had a single injury to personnel or evacuees. We treat every Dollar, Euro, Zloty, and Hryvnia with the greatest urgency in deploying. There is no amount too small to make a difference, and we won't advertise an " 'as low as' $3 per evacuee" number, as others have, that is misleading and doesn't really mean anything.

What's the price per mission?

Our cost-per-evacuee is roughly $40, lower than many larger aid organizations and on a shoestring and improvised budget. And we lower that cost every day. It reflects not only petrol and repairs to vehicles, but all expenditure: including investments/sunk costs such as our 500L fuel canister reserve.

Why does it seem the cost is so high per-evacuee?

Ours are further, significantly more dangerous rescue & evacuation missions than most other organizations are conducting or are willing to conduct. We opperate within range of Russian artillery and other hazards. Your money will be well spent.

Are there other organizations like yours that I can give money to?

Yes! Please reach out to us. There are other great organizations out there that we partner with (and others that we don't, but that we still value). We are happy to consult with you and put you in touch with them.
